M.Tech Thesis

Title: Modelling and Analysis of Scatterer Interference in a Terahertz Networks

  • The aim of this thesis is to analyze coverage probability, SINR, distribution of scatterer and Base Station(BS) interference at the user by following different approaches using stochastic geometry framework.

  • Here we incorporated system model with impact of blockages, molecular noise, thermal noise, antenna beam misalignment methods, bounded pathloss model, highly directional antennas which are very crucial in determining scatterer and BS Interference.

  • We proved that results of coverage probability and SINR that we have achieved for one of the derived analytical approximate approaches are similar to that of exact simulation approach.
